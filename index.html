Mental health Chatbot and Journaling Mobile App

Tech stacks: Flutter, Flask, MySQL

I took the helm of my team in developing a mental health chatbot and journaling app, leveraging Flutter for the front end and Flask for the backend. Our chatbot utilized the DASS-21 dataset and a Support Vector Machine model to predict individuals' mental states, ranging from depression, anxiety, to stress, along with their severity levels from low to high. While the machine learning model has not been fully integrated into the app, this stands as a potential future task. The app provided a platform for users to journal their thoughts and feelings, while the chatbot offered personalized support and guidance based on their mental health assessments. Our collaborative effort aimed to provide accessible mental health resources and support, utilizing innovative technology to enhance well-being.

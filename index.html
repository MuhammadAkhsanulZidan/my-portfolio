Tech stacks: Java, SQLite

Note Taking Android App

Tech stacks: Java, SQLite

I took the initiative to develop an innovative Android note-taking application reminiscent of traditional writing on a book, utilizing Java and SQLite for its development. This versatile app allows users to import PDF files and images, enabling them to seamlessly integrate external content into their notes. With the added feature of handwriting recognition, users can write directly on their device screen using either a hand or stylus pen, enhancing the app's usability and flexibility. Additionally, the app supports text input, providing users with multiple options for capturing their thoughts and ideas effortlessly.
